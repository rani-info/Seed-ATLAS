What this is

This page is a lightweight demo for a future seed platform focused on indigenous seeds, in-situ conservation, and incentive mechanisms that keep agrobiodiversity alive in real fields.

Indigenous seeds

Local names, traits, origin stories, and community knowledge (with consent).

In-situ conservation

Tracking cultivation status, threats, and farmer-led conservation practices.

Incentives

Identifying what support actually helps: markets, recognition, inputs, insurance, etc.

Note: This is static demo data for now.

In-situ conservation: what we track

  • Current cultivation status: active, occasional, discontinued
  • Farming system: rainfed/irrigated/terrace/forest garden
  • Threats: market pressure, climate stress, seed replacement, loss of knowledge
  • Seed practices: saving, storage methods, exchange networks
  • Verification: self-reported vs observed vs lab-tested

Incentives: what actually helps

Market-based

Premium pricing, buy-back guarantees, local procurement, branding support.

Non-market

Recognition, community seed banks, input support, training, insurance.

Governance

Benefit-sharing pathways, consent management, safe data sharing.

Equity lens

Who benefits? Women seed-keepers, indigenous communities, smallholders.
